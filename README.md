  <p>let’s live suddenly without thinking</p>
  <br>
  <p>under honest trees,</p>
  <p>a stream</p>
  <p>does.the brain of cleverly-crinkling</p>
  <p>-water pursues the angry dream</p>
  <p>of the shore. By midnight,</p>
  <p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;a moon</p>
  <p>scratches the skin of the organised hills</p>
  <br>
  <p>an edged nothing begins to prune</p>
  <br>
  <p>let’s live like the light that kills</p>
  <p>and let’s as silence,</p>
  <p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;because Whirl’s after all:</p>
  <p>(after me)love,and after you.</p>
  <p>I occasionally feel vague how</p>
  <p>vague idon’t know tenuous Now-</p>
  <p>spears and The Then-arrows making do</p>
  <p>our mouths something red,something tall</p>
